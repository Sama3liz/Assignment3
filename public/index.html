Edison's Passion for Photography and Reading

My Journey with Photography

I’ve always been drawn to capturing the beauty around me through a camera lens. Whether it’s a candid moment or a picturesque landscape, photography allows me to freeze time. One of my most memorable photography experiences was during a hike up the mountains. As the sun began to set, the entire landscape turned golden, and I captured a shot that reminded me of nature’s raw beauty. That photo remains one of my favorites.

Why I Love Reading

Reading has always been my escape to different worlds and times. I once got so lost in a book during a train journey that I missed my stop! That’s the magic of a good book – it can transport you to another world, making time fly by. Whether it’s fiction or non-fiction, every book has something to teach me.

Photography and Reading: A Perfect Balance

Both photography and reading give me different perspectives – one through the lens of reality and the other through imagination. Together, they fuel my creativity and curiosity, helping me grow both personally and professionally. These two hobbies are an essential part of my life.
